Understanding Emotions

Students learn to recognize and name their feelings in order to better understand them.

Self-Regulation

We teach children how to choose and employ coping strategies, and to understand what their body needs depending on their feelings.

Healthy Friendships

Students practice empathy, communication, and cooperation to develop strong, positive relationships.

Perseverance & Growth

We encourage students to take risks, not give up when tasks are challenging, and to problem solve independently and with peers.

Learning Styles

Students explore how they learn best, building confidence and independence in their academic work.
